A B O U T

Alyssa

Founder and Educator

Beauty has always been my passion since I was 6 years old. I vividly remember the time when I used my mother's makeup when she is at work and she would scold me when I get caught. Then I regularly started wearing winged liner when I was in highschool. My classmates would also ask me to do theirs and I loved it!
Fast forward a few years and I found myself doing makeup for others as a freelancer until I found my way doing lash extensions as Mermaid Esthetics' first ever service in 2017! Since then, I have been growing my business slowly but surely. It has been rough as I had to stop for rougly 2 years and half due to COVID-19. I reopened on December 2021 and have been thriving ever since. I'm always grateful for everyone that helped me throughout this journey and I'm looking forward to meeting more people as I go through this path.
